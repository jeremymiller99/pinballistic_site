WELCOME TO YOUR POWER STATION

Travelers from all over the war-torn galaxy rely on stations like yours to generate energy through the power of pinball. In Pinballistic, each run is a new opportunity to upgrade pinballs, components, and the board itself to fuel the galaxy and make some cold hard credits.

Space is a dangerous place, and that's not even counting the warmongering oligarchies, pirates, and cultist fanatics all vying for control; you never know when an emergency could arise. However, as the banks will never fail to remind you, you have quotas to meet! Run out of pinballs, and your station is left to drift in the cold vacuum of space until repairs are made — destroying your upgrades in the process.

The galaxy may be at war, but as long as the ships fly across the stars they'll still need you. So what are you waiting for? Go Pinballistic!
